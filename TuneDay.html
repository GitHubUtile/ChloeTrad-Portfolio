TuneDay: Play Your Routine!

An AI powered playlist maker.

To motivate and embrace your daily tasks. Once given a length and some details regarding the music genre, TuneDay will generate a playlist for "Your 20 minutes daily morning run" for example.

The playlist can then be scheduled making your routine organised with music and energy. Playlists can also be shared, making implementing daily habits much easier!
