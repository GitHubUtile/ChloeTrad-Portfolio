My first 3D project

Done using Blender

This animation is inspired by Beirut in the 1960s, where trees and nature dominated the city. The tramway was still in use, and we could see light snow covering even the urban area. As my first 3D animation, I explored different modeling tools, textures, lighting setups, and animation techniques.
